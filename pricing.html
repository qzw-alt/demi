We're your independent guide to China's healthcare system. We don't provide medical treatment—we provide the expertise to navigate it.

🎯 What We Do (And Don't Do)

We are: Information guides and service coordinators. We know which hospitals excel at which treatments, and we help you get there.

We are NOT: A hospital or medical provider. We don't make treatment decisions, perform procedures, or interfere with your doctor-patient relationship. Treatment plans and costs are between you and the hospital.
