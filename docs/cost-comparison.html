🤔 Why Does China Cost Less?

1. Lower Operating Costs

  • Doctor salaries are reasonable relative to local cost of living
  • Hospital rent and operational costs are lower
  • Medical equipment procurement and maintenance costs less

2. Economies of Scale

  • Chinese hospitals perform high volumes of procedures
  • Standardized processes reduce costs
  • Localized supply chains

3. Government Support

  • Significant investment in healthcare infrastructure
  • Policies supporting medical tourism development
  • Streamlined medical approval processes

4. Quality Without Compromise

  • Same imported medical devices and implants
  • Highly experienced surgeons (often 500+ procedures/year)
  • Modern, JCI-accredited facilities
