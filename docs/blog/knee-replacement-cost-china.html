Types of Knee Replacement Procedures

1. Total Knee Replacement (TKR)

Replaces the entire knee joint with prosthetic components. Best for patients with severe arthritis or extensive joint damage affecting multiple compartments.

Cost in China: $8,000-12,000 | Recovery: 6-12 weeks

2. Partial Knee Replacement (PKR)

Replaces only the damaged portion of the knee, preserving healthy bone and ligaments. Best for patients with localized arthritis in one compartment.

Cost in China: $5,000-7,000 | Recovery: 4-8 weeks

3. Knee Resurfacing

A less invasive option that preserves more bone. Best for younger, active patients who want to maintain more natural knee function.

Cost in China: $6,000-9,000 | Recovery: 4-6 weeks
