🔬 Types of Dialysis

1. Hemodialysis (HD)

Hemodialysis filters blood outside the body using a machine. Blood is pumped through a dialyzer (artificial kidney) that removes waste and excess fluids.

  • Frequency: 3 times per week, 3-5 hours per session
  • Location: Dialysis center or home (with training)
  • Advantages: Professional monitoring, social interaction
  • Cost: $50-100 per session in China

2. Peritoneal Dialysis (PD)

Peritoneal dialysis uses the lining of your abdomen (peritoneum) to filter blood inside your body. A dialysis solution is placed in your abdomen through a catheter.

  • Frequency: Daily (CAPD) or overnight (APD)
  • Location: Home
  • Advantages: Flexibility, travel-friendly, preserve residual kidney function
  • Cost: $300-500 per month in China

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I travel to China while on dialysis?

Yes! Many international patients travel to China for dialysis. Simply contact the hospital 2-4 weeks in advance with your medical records and preferred dates.

What do I need to bring?

Bring your dialysis prescription, recent lab results, medication list, and any allergy information. The hospital will handle the rest.

Is the water quality safe?

Yes, Chinese dialysis centers use purified water that meets strict international standards (ISO 13959).

Can I switch between hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis?

Yes, your nephrologist can help you transition between modalities based on your lifestyle and medical needs.

What about emergency care?

Top hospitals have 24/7 emergency services and can handle dialysis-related complications.
