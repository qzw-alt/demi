Advanced Technologies Available

🎯 Intraoperative MRI

Real-time imaging during brain surgery ensures complete tumor removal while preserving healthy tissue. Available at Tiantan and Huashan hospitals.

🧭 Neuronavigation Systems

GPS-like guidance for surgeons to navigate precisely within the brain and spine, minimizing risk to critical structures.

🤖 Robotic Surgery

Advanced robotic systems assist in precise drilling, implant placement, and minimally invasive procedures.

🔬 Awake Brain Surgery

Patient remains awake during brain tumor removal to preserve speech and motor function. Specialized technique available at top centers.
