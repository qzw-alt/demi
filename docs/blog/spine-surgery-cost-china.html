Types of Spine Surgery Available in China

1. Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery (MISS)

Chinese hospitals offer advanced minimally invasive techniques including:

  • Endoscopic discectomy: Small incision, fast recovery
  • Microsurgical discectomy: Using operating microscope for precision
  • TLIF (Transforaminal Lumbar Interbody Fusion): Minimally invasive fusion

2. Spinal Fusion

For patients with instability, scoliosis, or severe degeneration. Chinese surgeons use advanced navigation and robotic assistance.

3. Disc Replacement

Artificial cervical and lumbar disc replacement surgery maintains mobility while relieving pain.

4. Scoliosis & Deformity Correction

Complex deformity correction surgeries with 3D navigation and intraoperative neuromonitoring.
