Good news: You can start with a simple Tourist Visa (L Visa)!

  • 🟢 Tourist Visa (L Visa) — Perfect for most treatments. You can enter China, see doctors, and even use your international insurance for direct payment. Only need to switch if hospitalization exceeds your visa duration.
  • 🟡 S2 Medical Visa — For extended hospitalization (>10 days). We can help you convert from L to S2 visa while you're already in China — no need to leave the country.

How it works:

  1. Apply for L Visa (easiest to get)
  2. Enter China and consult with hospitals
  3. If you need extended treatment, we help you convert to S2 visa locally

Insurance tip: International insurance works with L Visa too! The visa type doesn't affect your insurance coverage.
