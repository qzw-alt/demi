## 🎬 完整脚本

### 1. 开场（30秒）

**画面**：一位60多岁男性在公园慢慢走路，痛苦表情

**旁白**：
> "Meet Robert, a 65-year-old retired teacher. For five years, he lived with severe hip pain. Every step was a struggle. Playing with his grandchildren? Out of the question. Even simple walks became impossible."

---

### 2. 困境（45秒）

**画面**：美国医院外观、排队候诊

**旁白**：
> "Robert was told he needed hip replacement surgery. In his home country, the cost would be between $35,000 and $50,000. Even with insurance, he would still need to pay about $12,000 out of pocket. And the wait time? Over a year."

**画面**：Robert坐在家里，愁容

**旁白**：
> "Can you imagine living with this pain for over a year? Robert tried everything - pain medications, physical therapy, injections. Nothing worked."

---

### 3. 转折（30秒）

**画面**：电脑屏幕、搜索界面

**旁白**：
> "One day, Robert's daughter told him about medical options abroad. She found that hip replacement surgery in another country cost just $8,000 to $12,000 - a fraction of what it would cost at home."

**画面**：Robert疑惑的表情

**旁白**：
> "Robert was skeptical at first. 'Another country? Are the hospitals good? Is it safe?' These were the questions on his mind."

---

### 4. 决定（25秒）

**画面**：Robert在手机上查看医院信息

**旁白**：
> "After two weeks of research, Robert decided to take a chance. He contacted a top hospital in Beijing that had an international department with English-speaking staff."

**画面**：签证申请、机票

**旁白**：
> "Within three weeks, he had his medical visa. Within one month, he was on a plane."

---

**画面**：手术室画面
