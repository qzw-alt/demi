## 🎬 完整脚本结构

### 1. 开场（30秒）

**画面**：主人公日常生活（走路疼痛、行动不便）
**旁白**：
> "John was 62 years old. For years, he lived with hip pain. Simple activities like walking his dog or playing with grandchildren became unbearable. But what he discovered next changed his life."

**字幕**：
- John, 62 years old
- Hip replacement needed
- Living with pain...

---

### 2. 美国的困境（45秒）

**画面**：美国医院外观、排队候诊
**旁白**：
> "In the United States, John was told he needed hip replacement surgery. The estimated cost? $45,000. And the wait time? 4 months. His insurance would cover part of it, but John would still need to pay over $8,000 out of pocket."

**字幕**：
- Wait time: 4 months
- Cost: $45,000
- After insurance: $8,000+ copay

**画面**：家庭对话/愁容
**旁白**：
> "John worried about two things: the cost, and the long wait. Every day meant more pain."

---

### 3. 转折（30秒）

**画面**：搜索界面、电脑屏幕
**旁白**：
> "One day, John's son mentioned something he'd read online - medical tourism to China. World-class hospitals, trained American doctors, and costs that were a fraction of US prices."

**字幕**：
- "Medical tourism to China"
- "60-80% cheaper"
- "World-class hospitals"

**画面**：疑虑表情
**旁白**：
> "John was skeptical at first. 'China? Is it safe? Is the quality good enough?' These were the questions on his mind."

---

### 4. 决定（30秒）

**画面**：搜索医院、了解信息
**旁白**：
> "After weeks of research, John decided to take a chance. He contacted a hospital in Beijing that had an international department with English-speaking staff."

**字幕**：
- Researched hospitals
- Contacted international department
- Got a response within 24 hours

**画面**：签证申请
**旁白**：
> "Within two weeks, he had his medical visa, and within one month, he was on a plane to Beijing."

---

### 5. 中国体验（2-3分钟）

**画面**：医院外观、国际部大堂
**旁白**：
> "When John arrived at the hospital, he was surprised. The international department looked like a luxury hotel. Clean, modern, and most importantly, everyone spoke English."

**字幕**：
- Beijing [某] Hospital
- International Department
- English-speaking staff

---

**画面**：医生问诊、沟通
**旁白**：
> "His first consultation lasted 45 minutes - more time than he'd ever spent with a US doctor. The surgeon explained everything in detail, answered every question John had."

**字幕**：
- 45-minute consultation
- Detailed explanation
- All questions answered

---

**画面**：术前检查、病房环境
**旁白**：
> "The pre-surgery tests were thorough. His private room was comfortable, with WiFi, TV, and even a view of the city."

**字幕**：
- Private room with amenities
- Comprehensive tests

---

**画面**：手术室、康复过程
**旁白**：
> "The surgery was successful. Three days later, John was walking with a physical therapist. The care he received was incredible - nurses checked on him every hour."

**字幕**：
- Surgery: Successful
- Walking: Day 3
- 24/7 nursing care

---

### 6. 花费对比（1分钟）

**画面**：对比表格动画

| 项目 | 🇺🇸 美国 | 🇨🇳 中国 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 手术费用 | $45,000 | $6,500 |
| 等待时间 | 4个月 | 2周 |
| 住院天数 | 3天 | 5天 |
| 医生沟通 | 10分钟 | 45分钟+ |
| 总花费(保险后) | $8,000+ | $6,500 |

**旁白**：
> "Here's the breakdown. In the US, John would have paid over $8,000 after insurance, and waited 4 months. In China, the total cost was $6,500 - and he was treated within just 2 weeks."

---

### 7. 康复与感悟（30秒）

**画面**：康复锻炼、出院笑容
**旁白**：
> "Today, John is pain-free. He can play with his grandchildren again. His total journey - surgery, recovery, and even a bit of sightseeing in Beijing - cost less than the copay would have been in the US."

**字幕**：
- Pain-free today
- Back to normal life
- Total cost: Less than US copay

**画面**：受访微笑
**旁白**：
> "If I had to make the choice again, I would go to China without hesitation. The quality of care was outstanding."

---

### 8. 结尾（15秒）

**画面**：医院Logo + 网站
**旁白**：

**字幕**：
- [Website URL]

**画面**：结束画面
**旁白**：
> "Thanks for watching. See you in the next story."

## 📝 注意事项

1. **数据真实性**：费用数据需核实，最好用真实案例
2. **隐私保护**：用虚构人物或征得患者同意
3. **字幕**：必须加英文字幕（YouTube自动翻译）
4. **CTA**：结尾放网站但不强行推销
