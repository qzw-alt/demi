Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Yes, completely free. There's no catch — we receive valuable content that helps other patients, and you receive professional coordination services. Both sides benefit.

What if I change my mind about sharing my story?

You'll sign an agreement upfront, but we understand circumstances change. We ask for good faith participation, and all content is reviewed by you before publication.

Can my family be included in the story?

Absolutely. Family members often play a crucial role in medical travel decisions. Their perspective adds valuable context to your story.

What happens after I apply?

We'll review your application within 48 hours. If selected, we'll schedule a video call to discuss your specific needs and finalize the agreement.

Do I need to pay for anything?

Our coordination services are free. You'll still need to pay the hospital directly for your medical treatment, as well as your travel and accommodation costs.
