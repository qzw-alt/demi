## Humanizer 优化示例

### 原文（AI风格）
Real data, real savings. A coronary bypass surgery costing $89,094 in the United States can be performed for $14,000–$25,000 in China—with comparable 5-year survival rates of 92%. This comprehensive guide breaks down actual 2026 pricing for heart surgery and cancer treatment, helping you make informed decisions about your healthcare options.

Healthcare costs in the United States continue to lead globally, often leaving patients facing impossible financial choices. Meanwhile, China's healthcare system has evolved to offer world-class treatment at a fraction of the cost—without compromising quality.

### 优化后（更自然）
A coronary bypass costs $89,094 in the US. In China? $14,000–$25,000. And here's what surprised me: the five-year survival rates are nearly identical at 92%. I pulled together actual 2026 pricing data because I was tired of seeing vague claims about "medical tourism savings" without real numbers. This is what heart surgery and cancer treatment actually cost—and where your money goes further.

American healthcare is expensive. Everyone knows it. What's less obvious is that China has quietly built a system where you can get the same quality care for a fraction of the price. I spent weeks digging into the data, and the gap is larger than I expected.

### 修改说明

| 问题类型 | 原文 | 修改后 |
|---------|------|--------|
| 口号式开头 | "Real data, real savings" | 删除，直接上数据 |
| AI词汇 | "comprehensive guide" | 删除 |
| 填充语 | "helping you make informed decisions" | 改为个人动机 "I was tired of..." |
| 过度正式 | "continue to lead globally" | 简化为 "is expensive" |
| AI词汇 | "evolved to offer" | 改为 "has quietly built" |
| 无来源声明 | 无 | 添加 "I spent weeks digging into the data" |
| 破折号过度使用 | "—without compromising quality" | 改为独立句子 |

### 核心改进
1. **添加个人声音** - "surprised me", "I was tired of", "I spent weeks"
2. **具体化** - "vague claims" 替代泛泛而谈
3. **承认复杂性** - "What's less obvious" 替代简单对比
4. **节奏变化** - 短句开场，逐渐展开
5. **删除AI痕迹** - 去除 "comprehensive", "evolved", "world-class" 等AI高频词

### 完整文章优化建议

如需优化整篇文章，建议按以下步骤：
1. 逐段检查24种AI写作模式
2. 重点修改：
   - 删除 "comprehensive", "crucial", "testament" 等AI词汇
   - 简化 "highlighting/underscoring" 等-ing结构
   - 去除 "It's not just X, it's Y" 句式
   - 减少em dash (—) 使用
   - 添加具体个人经历或数据来源
3. 保持数据和事实准确性
4. 保留SEO关键词（heart surgery cost China, medical tourism等）
