## 🛫 Step 3: Visa Application

### Visa Type: S2 (Private Affairs) - Medical Purpose

This is the correct visa for medical treatment in China. S2 visa allows up to 180 days stay in China.

### Required Documents:

**1. Hospital Invitation Letter** ⭐
- You can contact the hospital directly to apply
- Or we can help arrange it for **$30**
- The letter confirms you've been accepted for treatment

**2. Your Passport**
- Must be valid for at least 6 months
- At least 2 blank pages

**3. Proof of Financial Means**
- Bank statement showing $10,000+ USD
- This covers medical expenses and living costs

**4. Medical Documents**
- Prepare all relevant medical records in paper format
- Bring your diagnosis, test results, and any relevant documents

### How to Apply:

1. Contact hospital for invitation letter (or pay us $30 to arrange)
2. Fill out the online application form: https://bio.visaforchina.cn
3. Book an appointment at your nearest Chinese embassy/consulate
4. Submit all documents
5. Wait 4-5 working days (or 2-3 for expedited)

### Visa Extension (If Needed):

If you need to stay longer in China:
- **Where to apply**: Local Public Security Bureau (Exit-Entry Administration)
- **When**: 7 days before your visa expires
- **Documents needed**: Passport, hospital letter, accommodation proof
- **Extension**: Up to 180 days additional
- **We can help** with this process for an additional fee
