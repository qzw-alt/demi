🔬 What is Cataract Surgery?

Cataract surgery is a procedure to remove the cloudy lens of the eye and replace it with an artificial intraocular lens (IOL). It's one of the most common and successful surgical procedures performed worldwide, with a 98% success rate.

Types of Cataract Surgery:

  • Phacoemulsification (Phaco): The most common technique, using ultrasound to break up the cloudy lens
  • Extracapsular Surgery: For advanced cataracts, removing the lens in one piece
  • Laser-Assisted Surgery: Advanced femtosecond laser technology for precise incisions
