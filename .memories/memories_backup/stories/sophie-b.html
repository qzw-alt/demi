👁️ Sophie's Vision Correction Journey

From Glasses to Perfect Vision in Shanghai

📝 Patient Profile

Name Sophie B.
Nationality France 🇫🇷
Age 32 years old
Occupation Graphic Designer
Condition High myopia (-8.00D), dry eye from contact lenses
Treatment SMILE laser eye surgery (both eyes)

🎯 The Challenge

Sophie had been wearing contact lenses for over 15 years, but her high myopia (-8.00D) and increasing dry eye symptoms were making them unbearable. She wanted a permanent solution.

In France, she consulted about laser eye surgery:

  • LASIK cost: €4,500 per eye (€9,000 total)
  • Waiting time: 3 months
  • Concerns about dry eye complications

The cost and waiting time were significant barriers. Sophie started researching alternatives abroad.

✈️ Choosing China

Through online research, Sophie discovered that China has some of the world's leading eye hospitals with advanced SMILE technology. The Fudan University Eye & ENT Hospital in Shanghai particularly caught her attention.

"I was impressed by their reputation and the fact that they perform thousands of these procedures annually," Sophie recalls.

She contacted us for assistance, and we helped her:

  • Schedule a consultation within one week
  • Arrange accommodation near the hospital
  • Prepare all necessary medical records
  • Coordinate with English-speaking staff

🏥 The Treatment Experience

Sophie traveled to Shanghai in January 2026. The entire experience exceeded her expectations:

"The surgery was incredibly fast—just 5 minutes per eye. I felt no pain, only slight pressure. The doctor explained every step in English, which made me feel completely at ease."

Treatment Details:

  • Hospital: Fudan University Eye & ENT Hospital
  • Procedure: SMILE (Small Incision Lenticule Extraction)
  • Surgery time: 5 minutes per eye
  • Recovery: 2 hours observation, then free to go
  • Follow-up: Next day check-up

🎉 The Results

Sophie's recovery was remarkably fast:

  • Day 1: Vision 20/25, slight foreign body sensation
  • Week 1: Vision 20/20, dry eye symptoms significantly improved
  • Month 1: Vision stabilized at 20/16, no need for artificial tears
"The surgery process was very fast, almost no discomfort. The day after surgery my vision was 20/20, and now I don't need glasses at all. The doctors were exceptionally professional and explained everything in detail."
💡 Key Benefits Sophie Experienced:
• 53% cost savings (€5,250)
• No waiting time—surgery within one week
• Advanced SMILE technology (less invasive than LASIK)
• Perfect vision without glasses or contacts
• Bonus: Explored Shanghai for 3 days!

💬 Sophie's Advice

For anyone considering medical tourism for eye surgery, Sophie offers this advice:

"Do your research on the hospital and technology. SMILE was perfect for my dry eye condition. The key is finding a hospital with extensive experience—my surgeon had performed over 10,000 SMILE procedures. Also, plan some time to enjoy the city. Shanghai was amazing!"
