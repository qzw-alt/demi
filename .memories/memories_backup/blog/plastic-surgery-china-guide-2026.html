✨ Popular Procedures

Double Eyelid Surgery (Asian Blepharoplasty)

Creates a visible eyelid crease. Techniques include incisional method (permanent), suture method (less invasive), and partial incision. Recovery: 1-2 weeks initial, 3-6 months final results.

Price: $1,500 - $3,000

Rhinoplasty (Nose Reshaping)

Reshapes nose for improved aesthetics and function. Types include augmentation, reduction, and revision surgery. Recovery: 1-2 weeks social activities, 6-12 months final shape.

Price: $3,000 - $6,000
