Types of IVF Treatment Available

1. Standard IVF

Traditional in vitro fertilization where eggs and sperm are combined in a laboratory dish.

2. ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection)

Single sperm injected directly into egg. Recommended for male factor infertility, low sperm count, or previous IVF failures.

3. PGD (Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis)

Genetic testing of embryos before transfer to screen for specific genetic conditions. Particularly valuable for couples with inherited conditions.

4. Egg Donation

Using donor eggs from screened, healthy young women. High success rates (60-70%) for women with diminished ovarian reserve or genetic concerns.

5. Embryo Donation

Using donated embryos from other couples. Most affordable option with good success rates.

6. Frozen Embryo Transfer (FET)

Using previously frozen embryos. More affordable and allows for natural cycle timing.

Your IVF Journey in China

Step 1: Initial Consultation (Remote)

Send your medical records. Our team will match you with the best fertility specialist for your case.

Step 2: Treatment Plan & Preparation

Receive your personalized treatment plan including timeline, costs, and medication list. Begin ovulation suppression medications before travel.

Step 3: Travel to China

Arrive in China on Day 2 of your menstrual cycle. Typical stay: 3-4 weeks for one IVF cycle.

Step 4: Treatment Phase

  • Days 1-5: Ovulation stimulation with daily injections
  • Days 6-10: Monitoring (ultrasounds & blood tests)
  • Day 12-14: Egg retrieval (sedation, 20 min procedure)
  • Day 1-5: Embryo development in lab
  • Day 5-7: Embryo transfer (painless, 5 min procedure)

Step 5: Pregnancy Test

Blood pregnancy test (hCG) 10-14 days after transfer. Receive results and post-transfer care instructions.

Step 6: Follow-up

If pregnant, continue with local OB-GYN care. We provide pregnancy support and documentation for your records.

FAQs

How long do I need to stay in China for IVF?

Plan for 3-4 weeks in China for one complete IVF cycle. This includes egg retrieval and embryo transfer.

Is IVF in China safe?

Yes. Top Chinese fertility centers are internationally accredited and follow strict protocols. Success rates are comparable to Western clinics.

Can I do multiple IVF cycles in China?

Absolutely. Many patients do 2-3 cycles. Costs are so affordable that many couples attempt 2-3 cycles for the price of one in the US.

Do I need a visa for IVF treatment in China?

Yes. We'll provide a medical visa invitation letter (M Visa) to support your application.

What's included in the IVF cost?

Typically includes: doctor consultations, ultrasound monitoring, egg retrieval, embryo culture, embryo transfer, and one year of embryo storage. Medications are usually separate.
