📋 What to Expect: Health Checkup Process

Before Your Visit:

Preparation Checklist

  • Fast for 8-12 hours (water allowed)
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before
  • No strenuous exercise 24 hours prior
  • Bring previous medical records and imaging
  • List current medications
  • Women: Schedule outside menstrual period
  • Wear comfortable clothing

During Your Checkup:

  1. Registration & Consultation (30 min): Medical history review, physical examination by physician
  2. Blood Tests (15 min): Comprehensive metabolic panel, lipid profile, liver/kidney function, tumor markers, hormone levels
  3. Urine & Stool Analysis (15 min): Routine urinalysis, occult blood testing
  4. Imaging Studies (60-90 min):
    • Chest X-ray or CT scan
    • Abdominal ultrasound (liver, gallbladder, pancreas, kidneys, spleen)
    • Thyroid ultrasound
    • Pelvic ultrasound (women) / Prostate ultrasound (men)
    • Optional: MRI, PET-CT for advanced screening
  5. Cardiac Evaluation (30 min): ECG, echocardiogram, stress test (if indicated)
  6. Specialty Consultations (60 min): Based on age, gender, and risk factors

Timeline:

  • Basic Package: 4-6 hours, same-day results
  • Comprehensive Package: 1-2 days, results within 3-5 days
  • Executive/VIP Package: 2-3 days, comprehensive report with follow-up consultation

🔬 Comprehensive Screening Components

Basic Health Screening ($200-400)

  • Complete physical examination
  • Blood pressure, height, weight, BMI
  • Complete blood count (CBC)
  • Blood glucose and HbA1c
  • Lipid profile (cholesterol, triglycerides)
  • Liver function tests (ALT, AST, bilirubin)
  • Kidney function tests (creatinine, BUN, uric acid)
  • Urinalysis
  • Chest X-ray
  • Abdominal ultrasound
  • ECG (electrocardiogram)

Comprehensive Executive Screening ($600-1,200)

Includes all Basic tests plus:

  • Extended blood panel (thyroid function, coagulation, inflammation markers)
  • Tumor markers (AFP, CEA, CA19-9, PSA for men, CA125 for women)
  • Hepatitis B & C screening
  • HIV and syphilis testing
  • Chest CT scan (low-dose)
  • Thyroid ultrasound
  • Carotid artery ultrasound
  • Bone density scan (DEXA)
  • Pulmonary function test
  • Ophthalmology exam (vision, intraocular pressure, fundoscopy)
  • Dental examination
  • Nutrition consultation

Premium VIP Screening ($1,500-3,000)

Includes all Executive tests plus:

  • 3.0T MRI (brain, spine, or joints as indicated)
  • PET-CT for cancer screening
  • Coronary CT angiography (heart)
  • Comprehensive tumor marker panel (20+ markers)
  • Genetic screening for hereditary conditions
  • Food and environmental allergy testing
  • Heavy metal screening
  • Hormone panel (comprehensive)
  • Gastroscopy and colonoscopy
  • Private VIP suite with dedicated nurse
  • Personalized health report with 5-year risk assessment
  • Follow-up consultation with senior physician

⚠️ Important Considerations

  • Language: Ensure your hospital has English-speaking staff or bring a translator
  • Medical Records: Request English reports for your home country physicians
  • Follow-up Care: Plan how to address any findings with your local doctor
  • Insurance: Check if your insurance covers international preventive care
  • Timing: Allow 3-5 days in China for comprehensive screening
  • Privacy: VIP packages offer enhanced privacy and discretion
