## 📝 内容创作关键词指南

### 博客文章标题模板

1. **费用对比型**: "[Procedure] Cost in China 2026: Save [X]% vs [Country] | Complete Guide"
   - 示例: Knee Replacement Cost in China 2026: Save 70% vs USA | Complete Guide

2. **指南型**: "How to [Action] in China: [Benefit] for [Audience]"
   - 示例: How to Get Cancer Treatment in China: World-Class Care at 70% Less Cost

3. **对比型**: "[Topic] in China vs [Country]: [Key Difference] Guide 2026"
   - 示例: IVF in China vs USA: Cost, Success Rates & Quality Guide 2026

4. **问题型**: "[Question]? [Answer] for [Audience]"
   - 示例: Is Surgery in China Safe? What International Patients Need to Know

### Meta Description模板

1. **交易型**: "Save [X]% on [procedure] in China. [Social proof]. [CTA]."
   - 示例: Save 70% on knee replacement in China. 500+ patients helped. Free hospital guide.

2. **信息型**: "Learn about [topic] in China. [Key benefit]. [Supporting detail]."
   - 示例: Learn about cancer treatment in China. Access top oncology hospitals. English-speaking specialists.
