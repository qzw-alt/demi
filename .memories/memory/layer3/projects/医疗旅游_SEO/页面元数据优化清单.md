## 图片优化清单

### 所有图片必须包含

```html
<!-- 示例 -->
<img 
  src="images/knee-surgery-china.jpg" 
  alt="Knee replacement surgery patient recovery room at Beijing Jishuitan Hospital"
  width="800"
  height="600"
  loading="lazy"
>
```

### Alt标签写作规范

- 描述图片内容 (不是堆砌关键词)
- 包含1-2个相关关键词
- 控制在125字符以内
- 避免"image of"或"picture of"

### 示例Alt标签

| 图片 | 好的Alt标签 | 差的Alt标签 |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| 医院外观 | Beijing Jishuitan Hospital main entrance, JCI accredited orthopedic center | hospital image |
| 手术室 | Modern operating room at Fuwai Hospital with advanced cardiac equipment | surgery room picture |
| 患者康复 | International patient recovering after knee replacement surgery in China | patient photo |
