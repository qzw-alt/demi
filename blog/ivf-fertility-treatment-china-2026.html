IVF in China 2026: Complete Guide to Fertility Treatment for International Patients

March 16, 2026 | Fertility Treatment | By China Hospitals Guide Team

After three failed IVF cycles in London that cost her £45,000, Sarah was ready to give up. Her fertility specialist mentioned China as a last option. "The success rates are comparable," he said, "but the cost is a fraction." Sarah flew to Shanghai skeptical but desperate. Her fourth cycle worked. She's now mother to twin girls. The total cost? £8,500 including flights and accommodation.

Important Legal Notice: China's IVF regulations require patients to be legally married heterosexual couples. Single women and same-sex couples cannot access IVF treatment in China. This article is intended for eligible married couples seeking affordable fertility options.

Complete IVF Package in China typically includes:
  • Initial consultation and fertility assessment
  • Ovarian stimulation monitoring (ultrasounds & blood tests)
  • Egg retrieval procedure
  • Laboratory fertilization (IVF or ICSI)

  • Pregnancy test and early monitoring

Top 5 IVF Centers in China for International Patients

1. Peking University Third Hospital (PUTH) Reproductive Medicine Center - Beijing

#1 IVF in China Birthplace of Chinese IVF JCI Accredited

Why patients choose it: PUTH is where China's first IVF baby was born in 1988. They perform over 20,000 IVF cycles annually with consistent top-tier success rates.

Specialties: Complex infertility cases, recurrent implantation failure, PGT (genetic testing), fertility preservation

Success rates: 55-60% for women under 35

Cost: $4,000 - $6,000 per cycle

International services: Dedicated international patient department, English-speaking doctors, visa invitation letters

2. Shanghai Ji Ai Genetics & IVF Institute - Shanghai

Top 3 IVF Genetic Testing

Why patients choose it: Ji Ai is renowned for advanced genetic screening and PGT-M/PGT-A. They're the go-to center for couples with genetic concerns.

Specialties: PGT (Preimplantation Genetic Testing), recurrent miscarriage treatment, egg/sperm freezing

Success rates: 50-58% for women under 35

Cost: $4,500 - $7,000 per cycle (includes basic genetic screening)

Notable: First clinic in China to offer PGT-M for single gene disorders

3. Sun Yat-sen University First Affiliated Hospital - Guangzhou

Top 5 IVF Southern China Hub

Why patients choose it: Sun Yat-sen is the largest fertility center in Southern China. They have extensive experience with Southeast Asian patients.

Specialties: Male infertility, severe endometriosis, advanced maternal age (40+)

Success rates: 48-55% for women under 35

Cost: $3,500 - $5,500 per cycle

Location advantage: Guangzhou has a large international community and excellent flight connections
