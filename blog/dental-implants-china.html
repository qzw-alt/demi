Types of Dental Implants Available

1. Single Dental Implant

Replaces one missing tooth. Includes implant post, abutment, and ceramic crown. Perfect for individual tooth replacement.

2. All-on-4® Dental Implants

Full arch restoration using only 4 implants. Same-day teeth replacement. Ideal for patients missing all teeth in one or both jaws.

3. All-on-6 Dental Implants

Full arch with 6 implants for added stability. Better for patients with moderate bone loss.

4. Zygomatic Implants

For patients with severe bone loss. Implants anchored in the cheekbone, avoiding need for bone grafting.

5. Mini Dental Implants

Smaller implants for narrow ridges or temporary solutions. Lower cost option.
