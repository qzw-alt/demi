## 🛫 Step 3: Visa Application

### Visa Type: S2 (Private Affairs) - Medical Purpose

This is the correct visa for medical treatment in China. It's different from tourist (L) or business (M) visas.

### Required Documents:

**1. Hospital Invitation Letter** ⭐ (Most Important)
- We can arrange this for you
- The letter must be from a Chinese hospital
- It confirms you've been accepted for treatment
- Cost: $30-40 (we can help)

**2. Your Passport**
- Must be valid for at least 6 months
- At least 2 blank pages

**3. Proof of Financial Means**
- Bank statement showing $10,000+ USD
- This covers medical expenses and living costs

**4. Medical Records from UK**
- Your chest pain diagnosis
- Any relevant test results
- Translated into Chinese (we can recommend translators)

### How to Apply:

1. Contact us to get the hospital invitation letter
2. Fill out the online application form: https://bio.visaforchina.cn
3. Book an appointment at your nearest Chinese embassy/consulate
4. Submit all documents
5. Wait 4-5 working days (or 2-3 for expedited)

### Important Notes:

- **Apply 1-2 months before** your planned travel date
- S2 visa allows **up to 180 days** stay
- You can extend your stay in China if needed

## 🏥 Step 6: At the Hospital

### Day 1: Registration and Initial Consultation

**Morning:**
1. Go to International Department (Jiguang Building)
2. Show your passport at registration
3. Get your patient card
4. Pay consultation fee (¥600-1200)
5. Wait for your turn (usually 30-60 minutes)

**Meeting the Doctor:**
- Bring all your UK medical records
- Describe your chest pain: when it started, what makes it worse, associated symptoms
- The doctor will examine you and order tests

### Common Tests for Chest Pain:

| Test | Purpose | Cost (International) |
|------|---------|---------------------|
| ECG | Check heart rhythm | $30-50 |
| Blood Tests | Heart enzymes, etc. | $50-100 |
| Chest X-Ray | Check lungs/heart size | $30-50 |
| Echocardiogram | Heart structure | $80-150 |
| CT Angiogram | Check coronary arteries | $300-500 |

### Afternoon (if tests ordered):

1. Complete all tests at designated locations
2. Return to doctor with results
3. Receive diagnosis and treatment plan
