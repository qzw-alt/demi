## 3. 页面元数据优化建议

### 3.1 首页 (index.html)

**当前Title**: Medical Tourism China | Cancer, Cardiac & Orthopedic Treatment Guide

**建议优化**:
```html
<title>Medical Tourism China 2026 | Save 50-80% on World-Class Healthcare</title>
<meta name="description" content="Plan your medical tourism to China in 2026. Save 50-80% on knee replacement, heart surgery, cancer treatment & more. Top JCI-accredited hospitals, English support. Free guide.">
```

**建议添加结构化数据**:
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "MedicalBusiness",
  "name": "China Hospitals Guide",
  "description": "Medical tourism guide for international patients seeking affordable healthcare in China",
  "url": "https://chinahospitalsguide.com",
  "telephone": "+44-7947-060056",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "addressCountry": "CN"
  }
}
```

### 3.2 费用对比页 (cost-comparison.html)

**当前Title**: Medical Procedure Cost Comparison: China vs US/UK/Europe

**建议优化**:
```html
<title>Medical Costs China vs USA 2026 | Save 50-85% on Surgery</title>
<meta name="description" content="Compare medical procedure costs: China vs USA/UK. Knee replacement $8K-12K (save 70%), heart surgery $12K-18K (save 85%). Real 2026 prices.">
```

### 3.3 博客文章通用模板

**建议Title格式**:
```
[主题] in China 2026: [价值主张] | [品牌名]
```

**示例**:
- Knee Replacement Cost in China 2026: Save 70% on Surgery | China Hospitals Guide

## 5. 技术SEO建议

### 5.1 结构化数据实施

建议为以下类型添加Schema.org标记:

1. **医疗机构** (MedicalBusiness)
2. **文章** (Article) - 所有博客文章
3. **FAQ页面** (FAQPage)
4. **服务** (Service) - 各套餐
5. **评价** (Review) - 患者故事

### 5.2 图片优化

- 所有图片添加描述性alt标签
- 压缩图片大小 (建议<100KB)
- 使用WebP格式
- 添加图片结构化数据

### 5.3 移动端优化

- 确保所有页面移动端友好
- 检查移动端加载速度
- 优化移动端CTA按钮大小
