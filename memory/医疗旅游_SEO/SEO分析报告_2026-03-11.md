## 3. 页面元数据检查详情

### 3.1 首页 (index.html)

```html
<!-- 当前 -->
<title>Medical Tourism China | Cancer, Cardiac & Orthopedic Treatment Guide</title>
<meta name="description" content="Save 50-80% on world-class cancer, cardiac & orthopedic treatment in China. 40+ top hospitals with English service, no waiting lists. Free hospital guide.">

<!-- 建议优化 -->
<title>Medical Tourism China | Save 50-80% on World-Class Healthcare</title>
<meta name="description" content="Save 50-80% on cancer, cardiac & orthopedic treatment in China. 40+ JCI-accredited hospitals, English service, no waiting. Free guide & consultation.">
```

**分析**: 当前Title 58字符，接近上限。建议精简至55字符以内。

### 3.2 博客首页 (blog/index.html)

```html
<!-- 当前 -->
<title>Medical Tourism Blog | China Hospitals Guide</title>
<meta name="description" content="Expert guides on medical tourism in China...">

<!-- 建议添加 -->
<meta name="keywords" content="medical tourism blog, china healthcare guide, surgery costs, hospital rankings">
```

**分析**: 缺少keywords标签，建议添加。

### 3.3 文章页面元数据模板

建议为所有博客文章使用以下模板：

```html
<title>[文章标题] | China Hospitals Guide</title>
<meta name="description" content="[150-160字符描述，含主要关键词]">
<meta name="keywords" content="[3-5个相关关键词]">
<link rel="canonical" href="https://chinahospitalsguide.com/blog/[article-slug].html">

<!-- Open Graph -->
<meta property="og:title" content="[文章标题]">
<meta property="og:description" content="[描述]">
<meta property="og:type" content="article">
<meta property="og:url" content="[完整URL]">
<meta property="og:image" content="[文章特色图]">
<meta property="article:published_time" content="[ISO日期]">
<meta property="article:modified_time" content="[ISO日期]">
<meta property="article:author" content="China Hospitals Guide">

<!-- Twitter Card -->
<meta name="twitter:card" content="summary_large_image">
<meta name="twitter:title" content="[文章标题]">
<meta name="twitter:description" content="[描述]">
<meta name="twitter:image" content="[文章特色图]">
```
