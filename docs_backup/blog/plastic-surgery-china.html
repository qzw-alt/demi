Popular Procedures Explained

Breast Augmentation

Breast augmentation is one of the most popular procedures among international patients. In China, you can choose from the latest implant options (silicone, saline, gummy bear) with board-certified surgeons. Average procedure time: 1-2 hours. Recovery: 4-6 weeks.

Liposuction

Advanced liposuction techniques including VASER and laser-assisted methods available. Can target multiple areas in one session. Average procedure time: 2-4 hours. Recovery: 2-4 weeks.

Rhinoplasty

Both open and closed rhinoplasty techniques available. Chinese surgeons are particularly skilled at creating natural-looking results that complement facial features. Average procedure time: 1.5-3 hours. Recovery: 2-4 weeks.

Facelift (Rhytidectomy)

From mini facelifts to full facelifts, Chinese hospitals offer various options including thread lifts, SMAS facelifts, and endoscopic techniques. Average procedure time: 3-5 hours. Recovery: 3-6 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is plastic surgery in China safe?

Yes, when performed at reputable hospitals with international patient programs. Look for JCI accreditation, surgeons with international training, and hospitals with dedicated international patient centers.

How do I choose the right surgeon?

We'll match you with surgeons based on your procedure, desired results, and budget. All our partner surgeons have proven track records with international patients.

What about language barriers?

International patient centers provide English-speaking coordinators, nurses, and surgeons. Communication won't be an issue.

How long do I need to stay in China?

Most procedures require 7-14 days including initial consultation, surgery, and follow-up appointments. Some procedures may require longer stays.

What happens if there are complications?

All procedures include post-operative care and follow-up. Our partner hospitals have clear protocols for addressing any concerns during your recovery.
