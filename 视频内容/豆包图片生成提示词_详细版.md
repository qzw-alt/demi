## 🖼️ 图表1：费用对比柱状图

### 基础信息
- **用途**：视频 3:00 左右 - 费用对比章节
- **尺寸**：1920x1080
- **格式**：横向柱状图

### 英文提示词
```
Create a professional horizontal bar chart comparing medical surgery costs between USA and China.

TITLE: "Hip Replacement Surgery Cost Comparison"
(Use bold, modern sans-serif font)

DATA TO VISUALIZE:
- United States: $40,000 (show as large red/coral bar)
- China: $10,000 (show as teal/green bar, much smaller)

COLOR SCHEME:
- USA bar: #E74C3C (red/coral)
- China bar: #11998E (teal)
- Background: #FFFFFF (white) or very light gray #F8F9FA
- Text: #2C3E50 (dark blue-gray)
- Accent: #F1C40F (gold for highlighting savings)

STYLE REQUIREMENTS:
- Clean, modern infographic design
- Large, bold numbers ($40,000 and $10,000)
- Country flags: 🇺🇸 for USA, 🇨🇳 for China
- Show "70% SAVINGS" badge with gold star
- Include hospital/medical cross icon
- Professional business style
- Minimalist design with plenty of white space
- Subtle shadow under bars for depth
- Chart should be easily readable when scaled down

LAYOUT:
- Title at top center
- Two horizontal bars stacked vertically
- Percentage savings badge on the right side
- Country flags next to labels
```

### 中文参考
创建专业的横向柱状图，比较美国和中国的髋关节置换手术费用。标题"髋关节置换手术费用对比"。美国$40,000（红色），中国$10,000（青色）。显示"节省70%"徽章。现代简约风格，白色背景。

## 🎨 通用设计原则

1. **字体**：使用无衬线字体（Sans-serif）
   - 标题：80-150px
   - 副标题：40-60px
   - 正文：30-40px

2. **留白**：保持30%以上的留白空间

3. **对比**：确保文字在背景上有足够对比度

4. **品牌色**：
   - 蓝色系：#1E3C72, #2A5298
   - 绿色系：#11998E, #38EF7D
   - 金色：#F1C40F
   - 红色：#E74C3C

5. **动画友好**：使用简单形状，便于后续添加动画
